Who We Are

Welcome to Dahlia Tea Co, a small, passionate, tea business located in Victoria, BC. We’re all about tea, but more importantly, we’re all about being socially conscious, staying true to our sustainability and ethical values, and sharing the love for floral and flavored teas.

Our goal is simple: to bring you the finest teas, sourced responsibly, and crafted with care.

Ethical & Sustainable Quality

We take pride in delivering exceptional teas that are ethically sourced and environmentally conscious. To that end we’ve partnered with a supplier who shares our commitment to these values, ensuring our teas are both high quality and responsible.

We believe that the best teas can only be crafted when everyone is invested in their product, community, and environment.
